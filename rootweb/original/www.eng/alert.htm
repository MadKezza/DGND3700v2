<p>The NETGEAR mobile broadband router must be configured after it is connected into your network. You can do this using the Setup Wizard -- where the wizard will attempt to make the connection to the Internet service through your ISP -- or you can configure the router manually. It is recommended that, if you aren't used to setting up Internet connections, you let the Setup Wizard do some of the work for you.
